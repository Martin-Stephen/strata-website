How it works

Three layers. One regulated rail.

An issuer plugs in. Strata wraps, tokenises, administers, distributes. A channel lists the fund. Retail buys a unit. The middle is regulated, transparent, and tax-efficient — that's the whole product.

The three layers operate independently. Strata carries no credit risk; the issuer carries no compliance or distribution burden; the channel carries no product origination.

For the issuer

From NDA to first live fund.

A mid-tier credit manager (A$100M–A$2B AUM) can plug in within 4–6 months. Faster — around 2–3 months — if the manager already operates a retail-registered PDS.

Phase 02

Phase 03

Launch

Glossary

The ten terms you'll see everywhere.

If you're new to NZ/AU fund infrastructure, these are the ones worth knowing.

PIEPortfolio Investment Entity — an NZ tax wrapper that caps an investor's marginal rate at their PIR (10.5 / 17.5 / 28%). Strata issues PIE-compliant units.
MISManaged Investment Scheme — the NZ regulatory category Strata operates under. Licensed by the FMA.
MIT / AMITAustralian Managed Investment Trust / Attribution MIT — the AU equivalent of the PIE. Strata's architecture maps into MIT.
Master trustA parent trust that holds multiple sub-trusts. Adding a fund = adding a sub-trust, not a new licence application.
Sub-trustA ring-fenced trust for a single fund. Its own NAV, its own PDS, isolated from other Strata funds.
Tokenised unitA unit in a sub-trust recorded on a digital ledger. Enables programmable transfer, automated distributions, secondary-market liquidity.
Secondary marketPeer-to-peer transfer of units between investors. Strata's is guarded by NAV windows and issuer-set liquidity rules.
FMA sandboxA Financial Markets Authority exemption notice allowing regulated activity under bespoke conditions. Strata's runs 1 Dec 2025 – 30 Nov 2027.
REP 820ASIC's Nov 2025 private-credit surveillance report — raised the AU retail-compliance bar. Strata's transparency rails are designed to clear it.
bpsBasis points. 1bp = 0.01%. 25bps = 0.25%. The unit Strata prices administration fees in.
