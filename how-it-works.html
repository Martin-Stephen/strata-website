How it works

Three layers. One regulated rail.

A licensed credit manager plugs in. Strata wraps the fund, tokenises units, administers NAV + distributions + tax, and lists through retail channels. The middle layer is regulated, transparent, and tax-efficient — that's the product.

§ 01The stack

Issuer → Strata → Channel.

Three layers, operating independently. The issuer carries credit risk and origination. Strata carries the wrapper. The channel carries the retail surface.

No layer leaks into another. That is what makes the product safe to plug into, and is what keeps Strata out of competing with its customers.

Layer 03

Channel

Retail distribution

Sharesies self-select (in discussion), Snowball Effect, Kernel, InvestNow, HUB24 / Praemium adviser tier, or the direct Strata app.

Layer 02

Strata

Regulated rails

Master trust + sub-trusts, tokenised unit ledger, secondary market, multi-rate PIE tax engine, compliance surveillance, reporting.

Layer 01

Issuer

Licensed credit manager

The issuer's own AFSL / MIS, their loan book, their brand to retail, their credit underwriting. Unchanged by plugging in.

§ 04Scope

What Strata does — and doesn't.

Strata does

Regulated wrapperNZ MIS · FMA sandbox · PIE tax
Master trustDuplicate a deed to add a fund
Tokenised unitsDigital registry, programmable transfer
NAV + distributionsAutomated, scheduled or on-demand
Secondary marketPeer-to-peer within NAV windows
Multi-rate PIE tax28% / 17.5% / 10.5% handled per holder
ComplianceFair-dealing, disclosure, reporting
DistributionDirect app + third-party channels

Strata does not

Hold credit riskThe issuer's loan book stays with them
Originate loansWe don't lend. Issuers lend.
Underwrite borrowersThe issuer's credit team, not Strata's
Guarantee returnsNo capital protection. No implied yield.
Provide adviceWe are not an adviser
Custody directlyRegulated custodian per fund
Compete with issuersWe do not run a competing fund
Gate by adviserRetail-direct is the design
§ 05Glossary

Plain-language reference.

The ten terms you'll see everywhere on this site.

PIEPortfolio Investment Entity — NZ tax wrapper capping tax at the investor's PIR (10.5 / 17.5 / 28%).
MISManaged Investment Scheme — NZ regulatory category Strata operates under.
MIT / AMITAustralian Managed Investment Trust / Attribution MIT — the AU equivalent of PIE.
Master trustParent trust holding multiple sub-trusts. New fund = new sub-trust, not new licence.
Sub-trustRing-fenced trust for a single fund. Own NAV, own PDS.
Tokenised unitA unit recorded on a digital ledger — programmable transfer, automated distributions.
Secondary marketPeer-to-peer transfer between investors within NAV windows.
FMA sandboxAn FMA exemption notice permitting defined activity under specific conditions.
REP 820ASIC's Nov 2025 private-credit surveillance report. Raised the retail-compliance bar.
bpsBasis points. 1bp = 0.01%. The unit Strata prices admin fees in.
