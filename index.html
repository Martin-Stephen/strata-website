The category

The top-left quadrant is empty.

No multi-issuer, retail-direct, AU/NZ-regulated platform curates external private credit managers' products for sub-A$1K tickets. iCapital and CAIS are adviser-gated. Metrics and La Trobe are single-issuer. Squirrel is peer-to-peer.

Retail · sub-A$1K Wholesale · HNW

Two retail-first AU fractional plays (BrickX, DomaCom) have failed this decade. The AU private-credit managers above A$2B AUM compete with Strata's users — they will not build rails for their smaller peers. Global rails (iCapital, CAIS, Moonfare) are adviser-gated and US/EU-native.

Meanwhile ASIC's REP 820 (Nov 2025) raised the retail-compliance bar that mid-tier managers (A$100M–A$2B AUM) can't clear alone. That gap is Strata's wedge.
