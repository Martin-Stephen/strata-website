Legal · Privacy

Privacy policy.

Last updated 18 April 2026. Strata is committed to protecting your privacy under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020. This policy tells you what we collect, why, how long we keep it, and what your rights are.

Who we are

Strata is a trading name of Namaco Ltd, a company registered in New Zealand. Our privacy officer is the founder, Martin van Blerk. You can reach us at martin@namaco.com.

What we collect

  • Waitlist data. When you join the investor waitlist, we collect your name, email address, optional investment-size range, and residency (NZ / AU / other).
  • Contact data. When you email us or book a call, we collect the message you send and any information you include (e.g. your organisation, role).
  • Analytics data. We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-first, cookieless analytics tool. It records the page URL, a pseudonymised country, referrer, browser, and device type. It does not identify individual visitors and does not set cookies.
  • Form metadata. Form submissions pass through a third-party form service for delivery to our inbox. We do not store form data anywhere other than our email inbox.

What we do not collect

  • We do not set marketing or tracking cookies.
  • We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar third-party trackers.
  • We do not collect your IP address in identifiable form.
  • We do not collect phone numbers or payment details on the waitlist.

Why we collect it

  • To email you when the first fund on Strata's rails goes live.
  • To respond to your enquiry.
  • To understand, in aggregate, which parts of this site are useful.
  • To comply with NZ and AU regulatory obligations (AML/CFT, fair-dealing, record-keeping).

Who we share it with

We do not share your personal data with credit managers, channel partners, or any other third party without your explicit consent. The only third parties that process your data on our behalf are:

  • Our email provider (Google Workspace) — to receive and reply to your messages.
  • Our form delivery provider — to deliver form submissions to our inbox.
  • Our analytics provider (Plausible) — to measure aggregate site traffic.

We may be required to disclose data to the New Zealand Financial Markets Authority, the New Zealand Police, Inland Revenue, or a regulator in another jurisdiction where the law requires us to do so.

How long we keep it

Waitlist data is retained until you unsubscribe or the waitlist is closed. Contact emails are retained for seven years to meet record-keeping obligations. Analytics data is aggregated and retained indefinitely in non-identifying form.

Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020 you have the right to:

  • Ask what data we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct it.
  • Ask us to delete it (subject to our record-keeping obligations).
  • Complain to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner at privacy.org.nz.

Email martin@namaco.com to exercise any of these rights. We will respond within 20 working days.

Changes

If we change this policy we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of the page. Material changes will be announced to anyone on the waitlist by email.
