Capital-loss risk

Private credit carries capital-loss risk. The value of a unit in a private-credit fund can go down as well as up. Loans inside a fund may default; collateral may not recover full value; distributions may be reduced or suspended; redemption may be delayed or limited. Private credit is not a bank deposit and is not covered by a government guarantee.
