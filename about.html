About

Second-time operator. First-time category.

Strata was founded in Auckland in 2024. The platform is built for a category that has no incumbent in AU/NZ — regulated, retail-direct private-credit infrastructure. Two years, $2.2M+, a full legal stack, and a regulator positioned. The product is ready for the first live fund.

§ 01Founder
MV
Martin van Blerk
Founder · Auckland, NZ

Martin van Blerk

Martin founded Pixelmon in 2022, which did US$100M+ in primary sales in its first 24 hours. He exited his controlling stake to an Animoca-backed studio. Pixelmon taught him how to build, launch, and operate a regulated digital-asset product at scale — and what to avoid when doing it again.

He founded Strata in 2024 to solve the infrastructure gap he'd watched form in AU/NZ: institutional-grade private credit with no retail-distribution surface. Strata is his full-time focus. He's committed $2.2M+ of personal capital over 24 months and has not raised external equity.

Martin is co-founder of Project Saved, an anti-trafficking charity in Nepal. He lives in Auckland.
